Customer Pain Points

Insights into areas where customers face challenges

Platform: Website

Pain Point: Slow loading times during checkout.

Improvement Suggestion: Optimize page speed by compressing images and reducing server response time.

Platform: Mobile App

Pain Point: Difficulty navigating the menu on smaller screens.

Improvement Suggestion: Implement a more user-friendly dropdown menu with clear categories.

Platform: Call Center

Pain Point: Long waiting times to speak with a representative.

Improvement Suggestion: Increase call center staffing during peak hours.

Platform: Social Media

Pain Point: Delayed responses to customer inquiries.

Improvement Suggestion: Use automated chatbots to provide instant replies for common queries.
